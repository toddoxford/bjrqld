Personalized Service

When engaging BJR Queensland P/L to undertake your project, you will be dealing with the director and owner Brett Rogers, from the initial meeting right through until the completion of your project.

You will not be dealing with any sales people, middlemen or staff.
My personal role involves
  • Producing drawings
  • Attending meetings
  • Answering any queries
  • Liasing with other consultants

About

Brett Rogers started BJR Queensland P/L in 1997 as a building design company to service the public, consultants, and builders. During the past 20 years we have been involved in a wide range of projects

New residential homes Residential alterations/additions Townhouses Units Duplexes Decks & pergolas Multi million dollas residences Commercial alterations/additions Small shopping complexes Gyms Office fitouts

We are proud to be members of the Queensland Master Builders Association, and hold the following Q.B.C.C. No. 1001728 Licences in Building Design and Building.

What's The Cost

I do not work on a square meterage rate or percentage of project cost. I believe all projects in themselves are unique, and therefore will structure the cost accordingly. Some clients may just want a very basic set of plans for building approval and pricing, whilst others may require more detailed drawings and additional services. This means the best outcome for you in terms of service and price.

What We Can Arrange

During the preparation of your plans other consultants may be required such as

Engineer Soils Tester Building Certifier Surveyor Town Planner

I can arrange and liaise with any required consultant on your behalf and exchange all drawings and data in electronic format.

Lodging plans for building / development approval, and also arranging suitable builders for the purpose of tendering are another serivce I can provide.
